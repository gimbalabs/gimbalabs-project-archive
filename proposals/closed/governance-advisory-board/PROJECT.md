# Governance: Advisory Board

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
Currently, Gimbalabs lacks a comprehensive policy framework, which can lead to confusion, inefficiencies, and potential risks. This proposal aims to develop policies that will provide a solid foundation for Gimbalabs' operations and ensure a safe, inclusive, and productive environment for all stakeholders.

## Summary
Proposal to establish a Community Advisory Board at Gimbalabs to develop a comprehensive policy framework, ensuring safe, inclusive, and effective operations while addressing governance, safety, and stakeholder engagement over the next three months.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Community Advisory Board (AB) purpose is to provide safe space for community members to express their opinions. This will ensure key stakeholder opinions are considered while maintaining confidentiality of their opinions.

### 2. Background or Rationale
Gimbalabs aims to provide open, replicable safe spaces for individuals and organizations to learn, explore, and empower themselves. To ensure the smooth operation and growth of Gimbalabs, it is essential to establish clear policies that outline the guidelines, rules, and procedures for various aspects of the organization. Currently, Gimbalabs lacks a comprehensive policy framework, which can lead to confusion, inefficiencies, and potential risks.

### 3. Intended Outcome
The intended outcome of this proposal is to develop a comprehensive policy framework that outlines the guidelines, rules, and procedures for Gimbalabs' operations, ensuring a safe, inclusive, and productive environment for all stakeholders.

### 4. Details of the Proposal
The policy development process will involve:
- Conduct a thorough review of existing policies, procedures, and guidelines
- Identify gaps and areas for improvement
- Develop new policies and procedures to address the identified gaps
- Review and refine the policies with input from stakeholders
- Finalize and implement the policies

The policies will cover areas such as:
- Governance and decision-making
- Membership and participation
- Safety and security
- Intellectual property and confidentiality
- Data management and privacy
- Financial management and accountability
- Communication and conflict resolution

### 5. Impact and Benefits
The development of a comprehensive policy framework will have a significant impact on Gimbalabs, including:
- Improved clarity and consistency in operations
- Enhanced safety and security for stakeholders
- Increased transparency and accountability
- Better management of risks and conflicts
- Improved decision-making and governance
- Enhanced reputation and credibility

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
- Timeliness: Are the policies developed and implemented within the proposed timeline?
- Quality: Are the policies comprehensive, clear, and effective in guiding Gimbalabs' operations?
- Stakeholder engagement: Are stakeholders engaged and involved in the policy development process?
- Impact: Do the policies lead to improved operations, safety, and accountability?

### 7. Timeline
The policy development process will take place over 3 months:
- Month 1: Review of existing policies and procedures, identification of gaps, and development of new policies
- Month 2: Review and refinement of policies with stakeholder input
- Month 3: Finalization and implementation of policies

### 8. Resources Required
- Dedicated personnel (policy developer, reviewers, and stakeholders)
- Access to relevant documentation and information
- Technology and tools for collaboration and communication

### 9. Responsibilities
- Policy Developer: Responsible for developing and refining the policies
- Reviewers: Responsible for reviewing and providing input on the policies
- Stakeholders: Responsible for providing input and feedback on the policies

### 10. Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies
**Potential risks:**
- Delays in the policy development process
- Insufficient stakeholder engagement
- Inadequate resources

**Mitigation strategies:**
- Regular check-ins and progress updates
- Active stakeholder engagement and communication
- Contingency planning for potential delays or resource constraints

### 11. Review and Adaptation Plan
The policies will be reviewed and adapted as necessary, with a formal review process scheduled for every 12 months. The review process will involve:
- Evaluation of the effectiveness of the policies
- Identification of areas for improvement
- Revision and refinement of the policies as necessary
