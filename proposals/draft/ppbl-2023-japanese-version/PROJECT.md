# Keep the Gimbalabs Plutus PBL 2023 - Japanese version alive

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver

### Current Situation
Gimbalabs Plutus Project-Based Learning 2023 - Japanese version is now available for study by Japanese students. It is one of the first Cardano materials from the western hemisphere to be translated into Japanese. Its existence is proof of Gimbalabs' commitment to global education. The planning stage of PPBL 2024 has highlighted the weaknesses of PPBL 2023, which puts the survival of the Japanese version of PPBL 2023 at risk.

### Effect on the Organization
In addition to having required a considerable amount of time and effort, and to be considered an asset of Gimbalabs, the disappearance of PPBL 2023 Japanese version without the existence of a PPBL 2024 translated into Japanese would mean the disappearance of the nascent bond that is being created with the Japanese community and the East Asia region in general, and in general, of a public resource that has been put at the service of the Community.

### Organizational Need
Gimbalabs needs to keep the Japanese version of PPBL 2023 alive until a version of PPBL 2024 appears to replace it, similar to book versions that still exist even though new versions of it have appeared.

### Impact of Attending to the Need
- The Gimbalabs Plutus PBL 2023 Japanese version will continue to be proof of Gimbalabs' commitment to global education.
- The existence of the PPBL 2023 Japanese version can potentially encourage local communities in East Asia (and other regions) to take up the same challenge of translating the PPBL into their native language. It should be remembered that there are still pending promises to be fulfilled by communities such as Indonesia and Vietnam, which started the translation process and abandoned it halfway through. The disappearance of the PPBL 2023 Japanese version would eliminate the evidence that translating material of the magnitude of the Gimbalabs PPBL is possible.
- The Cardano development community sees Gimbalabs as an organization that is willing to take aggressive steps in the areas of onboarding developers around the world. The Gimbalabs PPBL 2023 Japanese version is part of that kind of aggressive step.
- Japan is being relevant this year at the community level, and at the level of potential partnerships between Gimbalabs/Andamio and Japanese entities. The PPBL 2023 Japanese version has helped to open up conversations with a community that is not easy to navigate. The PPBL 2023 Japanese version will continue to serve its function of opening up conversation spaces while its Japanese-translated 2024 successor arrives to replace it.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Keep the Gimbalabs Plutus PBL 2023 - Japanese version alive until a Japanese translation of the PPBL 2024 emerges to replace it.

### 2. Background or Rationale
The Gimbalabs Plutus PBL 2023 Japanese version represents a tie to the Japanese Cardano community and a commitment to the entire East Asia region. At the same time, it is evidence of the explorations Gimbalabs is willing to pursue to provide opportunities for access to education and open participation free of gatekeepers.

The planning stage of PPBL 2024 has highlighted the weaknesses of PPBL 2023 without highlighting its strengths, which include still being a viable tool for engaging students with Cardano technology and sparking their curiosity enough to encourage them to take their first steps in the Cardano development community.

The driver of the proposal "Deliver Plutus PBL 2024" states: *"understand the architecture of an application built on Cardano by implementing the validator logic and corresponding transactions through a series of applied examples"*. Although under discussion for the current analysis performed in the PPBL 2024 planning process, the PPBL 2023 version still provides such an understanding.

At the very least, it can be expected that the Japanese version of PPBL 2023 will continue to fulfill its role of providing education that allows developers and entrepreneurs to make informed decisions about what is possible to deploy on the Cardano blockchain and adjust their expectations accordingly: our experience at Gimbalabs can testify on initiatives that are formulated around technologies such as vending machines or Atala Prism-based certifications that are not grounded and do not connect the dots properly given an inadequate understanding of what is possible.

Eliminating the Japanese version of PPBL 2023 without there still being a Japanese version of PPBL 2024 to relieve it of its duties will again leave a void that only Gimbalabs has so far bothered to fill: not even the educational divisions of the founding entities have taken seriously the task of encouraging local communities to plant their own seeds.

### 3. Intended Outcome
Keep alive and accessible the Japanese version of Gimbalabs Plutus PBL 2023, which can be accessed through the URL https://ja.plutuspbl.io/, until a Japanese-translated version of Gimbalabs Plutus PBL 2024 appears to replace it, on the principle that currently, Plutus PBL 2023 is still competent to engage developers with the discovery of the architecture of a dapp built in Cardano.

### 4. Details of the Proposal
Unique action: keep the current Japanese version of Gimbalabs Plutus PBL accessible through the URL https://ja.plutuspbl.io/, until a Japanese version 2024 comes out to relieve it.

### 5. Impact and Benefits
- The Gimbalabs Plutus PBL 2023 Japanese version will continue to be proof of Gimbalabs' commitment to global education.
- The existence of the PPBL 2023 Japanese version can potentially encourage local communities in East Asia (and other regions) to take up the same challenge of translating the PPBL into their native language. It should be remembered that there are still pending promises to be fulfilled by communities such as Indonesia and Vietnam, which started the translation process and abandoned it halfway through. The disappearance of the PPBL 2023 Japanese version would eliminate the evidence that translating material of the magnitude of the Gimbalabs PPBL is possible.
- The Cardano development community sees Gimbalabs as an organization that is willing to take aggressive steps in the areas of onboarding developers around the world. The Gimbalabs PPBL 2023 Japanese version is part of that kind of aggressive step.
- Japan is being relevant this year at the community level, and at the level of potential partnerships between Gimbalabs/Andamio and Japanese entities. The PPBL 2023 Japanese version has helped to open up conversations with a community that is not easy to navigate. The PPBL 2023 Japanese version will continue to serve its function of opening up conversation spaces while its Japanese-translated 2024 successor arrives to replace it.

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
- Number of students consulting the Japanese version of Gimbalabs PPBL 2023.
- Number of partnerships and collaborations that the Japanese version of Gimbalabs PPBL 2023 encourages.
- Formation of new study groups

### 7. Timeline
Make a commitment upon approval of this proposal to keep the Japanese version of Gimbalabs PPBL 2023 alive and accessible **throughout the year 2024**, or until a Japanese version of PPBL 2024 emerges to replace it.

### 8. Resources Required
- Budget to keep the content of the URL https://ja.plutuspbl.io/ deployed in its current conditions.
- Budget to maintain ownership of the domain https://ja.plutuspbl.io/

### 9. Responsibilities
- Sebastian Pabon, in charge of the PPBL 2023 Japanese translation work.
- James Dunseith, responsible for enabling the `language-japanese` branch in the main PPBL 2023 repository and authorizing the deployment of the Japanese content.

### 10. Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies
**Risk:** The existence of an outdated version of the PPBL while an updated version is available for consultation, can be detrimental to Gimbalabs in terms of know-how and goodwill.

**Mitigations:**
1. We have yet to wait for the PPBL 2024 version to be released in its entirety.
2. The PPBL is an educational material and like all educational material, versions prior to the latest version are still in circulation.
3. A notice about the PPBL being in process of updating may be placed on the 'main' page of the Japanese version of the PPBL.

### 11. Review and Adaptation Plan
The first review of the relevance of the Japanese version of PPBL 2023 could be conducted once the construction of PPBL 2024 has been completed. After this first review, subsequent reviews of the same decision could be conducted quarterly.
