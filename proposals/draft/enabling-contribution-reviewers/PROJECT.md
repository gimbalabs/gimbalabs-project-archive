# Enabling Contribution Reviewers

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
At https://gimbalabs-prototype.andamio.io/, Contributors can commit to projects. After they do, Reviewers are responsible for approving that commitments have been met. When they do, Reviewers build a transaction that releases the Contributor's access token and rewards. Gimbalabs needs Reviewers who understand the technical aspects of the Reviewer role, and also who play a supporting role in projects. By sharing the Reviewer role among Gimbalabs community members, we will advance into timely explorations of governance. We'll also have to create internal documentation and processes that might eventually be worth sharing with other organizations.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Enabling Contribution Reviewers

### 2. Background or Rationale
- Account for learning curve
- Account for R&D and evolving the system

### 3. Intended Outcome
- Internal documentation of Review process
- Reviewers approve projects in contributor prototype
- New ideas → feature requests and next proposals

### 4. Details of the Proposal
Contributors can commit to projects. After they do, Reviewers are responsible for approving that commitments have been met. When they do, they build a transaction that releases the Contributor's access token and rewards. It's a big responsibility for two reasons:
1. Contributors are waiting for Reviewers to act
2. By approving a commitment, a Reviewer is helping to build the on-chain track record of the Contributor

This can be a very meaningful role. So what does it entail?
1. Technical knowledge: how does the Andamio Contributor Platform work? What can Reviewers do? What should they be able to do?
2. Facilitation skills + feedback strategies: how can reviewers contribute to the success of each commitment?
3. Conflict resolution: what happens when people disagree whether work is complete?

### 5. Impact and Benefits
- shared governance
- quick, responsive feedback + rewards

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
