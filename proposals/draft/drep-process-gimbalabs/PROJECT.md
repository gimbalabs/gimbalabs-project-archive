# A DREP process for Gimbalabs Community DREP role

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
Current Situation: GL is figuring out the best way to be a drep for this community. The current process is not ideal and we have the opportunity in experimenting with different processes. In solving this meaningful problem of how to be a DREP would empower individuals and organizations in Cardano and other constituencies.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
A DREP process for Gimbalabs Community DREP role

### 2. Background or Rationale
Related proposals:
- GL proposal: [DRep Education + Current Votes](https://gimbalabs.notion.site/DRep-Education-Current-Votes-1a00db6d1d5d804f8457fa89522ddd80?pvs=4)
- GL Drep proposal: [Original Proposal: Gimbalabs DRep](https://gimbalabs.notion.site/Original-Proposal-Gimbalabs-DRep-1290db6d1d5d80e0abaadd516aff722f?pvs=4) (Record organizational driver workshop done Oct 2024)
- Another Governance (of GL) proposal: [Governance Advisory Board](https://www.notion.so/gimbalabs/Governance-Advisory-Board-b500a0a823ff422cbfa18d41f2ce5862?pvs=4)

**Needs and wants of GL:**
- GL needs a strong Cardano as it supports us with opportunity and funding
- GL needs an informed membership in regards to GA and their effects
- GL is an institution of Cardano (Provides education and networking - needing to look after its own interest and its domains: education, governance, networking, building)
- The need for constituency education - unbiased explanation of the Governance action proposals

### 3. Intended Outcome
- A commitment by a community member to champion the process of GL DREP
- A documented, transparent process for the GL DREP to follow and maintain
- Coordinated effort by the community around Governance Actions (GA)
- GL becomes a trusted institution for DREP delegation and delegate communication
- GL acts with integrity according to our primary driver and beyond

**Unwanted outcomes to avoid:**
- Being too political
- Fractioning the community
- Overcomplicating the process or knowledge transfers
- Unnecessary administrative overhead (costs, time, maintenance)

### 4. Details of the Proposal
This is a proposal to create/change the processes for, and the formalization of, the DREP role and Governance actions in Gimbalabs. It seeks to receive a commitment from a person who will coordinate and document the DRep processes for Gimbalabs.

**SUGGESTED ACTIONS AND PROCESSES FOR THE GL-GOV-ROLE:**

- Create DREP / governance category and role on the GL discord
  - Requires certain Discord permissions
  - A forum, a Discord channel, to allow individual GA posts and threads
  - A general governance discussion channel, not Related to a specific GA (change current drep chat)
  - Change current Learn-Cardano-Governace to FAQ, or entry-type channel ('start here', resources)
  - Add channels as necessary as the processes are streamlined through trial and feedback
- Monitor web (gov.tools, X, etc.) for GA
- When necessary, create a new post in discord
  - notify @everyone - for community input and discussion
    - once vote is complete - discussion thread is locked and closed
    - collect viewpoints and data
  - Generate a poll (straw poll) x-number of days before community space (Open spaces Thursday)
  - Allow time before the vote - to give time to discuss, address concerns, read and understand
    - Current plan is to use an allotted window within Open Spaces
- **When necessary**, create a Notion Proposal (can happen in parallel)
  - Collect data, info, links, support for all options of vote (yes, no, abstain, ignore) **in one document**
    - **to ensure data-continuity, transparency, knowledge management...**
  - Identify the methods we use and provide **static URLs** to GL archives
  - Facilitate an Open Space discussion (with S3 consent or hybrid format)
  - Consent to a vote option (yes, no, abstain, ignore)
  - Include the rationale for other options not previously collected
- Close the process
  - Move the Notion proposal to 'done'
  - Archive a 'packet' on the Github - (Discord allowing .md makes it easier to copy/paste)
  - Terminate the discord posts, with potential pruning.

### 5. Impact and Benefits
- Innovate and streamline the current process
- An ideal would be that the entire community would have input and be aware of the governance actions
- Onboarding new community members - interested in the governance, and other - this could be an entrance / opportunity for new membership
- Provide real transparency - all community needs governance
- Allows participation by all interested parties within and without Gimbalabs

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
*How should these processes and roles be reviewed?*
- General feel
- Engagement of the community
- Value of the artifacts and documentation
- Value of the discussion (async and live)

### 7. Timeline
- Start: Mid-April 2025
- End: A continuing process, ending is... as long as we are DREP.
- Review and assessment: Every 3 months

### 8. Resources Required
What this proposal asks:
- Commitments from community to support and engage with the DREP process
  - A new role in discord for governance actions
- Champion to commit to managing the process
  - "A few minutes to read the governance actions and make a post in the discord channel every few days" - perhaps create a poll - creating a full proposal
  - And has the autonomy to exercise this process in any manner that benefits the community
  - A commitment from a person is required for this proposal to move forward and to be consented to
  - Community may also nominate any person for this role as long as that person accepts the commitment
- Person should document their processes for this role as a community/Gimbalab DRep
- Possibly an async process on Discord and where the community is updated in Open Spaces
  - possible future breakout rooms
- Reorganize Discord as necessary to encourage participation and share knowledge
- **Vote and write the rationale**
  - Includes context, dissenting views and other opinions
    - For example, "We are voting yes because.... And, we acknowledge the dissenting view of..."
- Structure needs to be transparent, available to the community and not rigid
  - emergence of governance actions and their processes to the Gimbalabs community (civics minded, collaborative in nature)

**Financial contributor rewards:**
- Option 3: Volunteer (at least for the first 3 month review period)

### 9. Responsibilities

**The GL community and its delegates:**
People interested in DREP voting or the general GL community:
- Monitor / engage with the DREP posts in the discord
- Attend meetings and participate in the consent process
- Provide input and opinions about the DREP vote

**A Gimbalabs DREP role:**
Minimum tasks:
- Do the actual vote transaction
- Write the rationale

Suggested tasks:
- Facilitate the process for community members - on Discord and at Spaces
- Synthesize the community input
- Document the process (Discord, Notion)
- Archive the documents (Github)
- Represent GL at DREP events and community projects as needed and agreed

### 10. Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies
- Biases
- Lack of participation from community and delegates
- Failure to vote
