# Hackathon!

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
The world is facing unprecedented environmental challenges, and the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) emphasize the need for urgent action to combat climate change and promote sustainable development. Meanwhile, the blockchain ecosystem is rapidly evolving, with platforms like Cardano emerging as leaders in the space. However, there is a lack of awareness and expertise among developers on how to leverage blockchain technology to address environmental challenges, particularly in the context of carbon trading.

**Effect:** This situation leads to a missed opportunity for the blockchain ecosystem to contribute meaningfully to the attainment of the SDGs, particularly Goal 13 (Climate Action). Furthermore, the lack of engagement from the developer community may hinder the adoption of blockchain-based solutions for carbon trading, ultimately slowing down progress towards a more sustainable future.

**Relevance:** As an organization committed to providing open, replicable safe spaces to learn, explore, and empower individuals and organizations to solve meaningful problems, Gimbalabs has a unique opportunity to respond to this situation. By hosting a hackathon focused on blockchain-based carbon trading, Gimbalabs can onboard existing developers to build projects that promote Cardano as a digital public good, while also contributing to the attainment of the SDGs. This aligns with Gimbalabs' mission and provides a chance to make a positive impact on the environment, while also showcasing the potential of blockchain technology to drive sustainable development.

**TLDR:** A SDG and digital public good hackathon for Cardano

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
"Cardano for Climate Action - enabling Carbon Credit Markets" Hackathon

### 2. Background or Rationale
Last year's [Cardano Summit hackathon](https://cardanofoundation.org/blog/cardano-summit-hackathon) demonstrated the potential for blockchain-based solutions to drive innovation and collaboration in the developer community. Building on this momentum, Gimbalabs proposes hosting a hackathon focused on blockchain-based carbon trading, with the goal of promoting sustainable development and contributing to the attainment of the SDGs. Highlight the technical challenges and developing solutions - in a concrete way. Moving the conversation from claims and philosophical approaches.

Solutions exist. But how to translate to Cardano and make better. How to make people want to use the solution.

### 3. Intended Outcome
The hackathon aims to bring together developers, designers, and experts in the field of carbon trading to build innovative blockchain-based solutions that promote sustainable development. The intended outcomes include:
- A minimum of 3 projects developed during the hackathon, with at least 1 project receiving funding or support for further development
- Increased awareness and expertise among developers on how to leverage blockchain technology to address environmental challenges
- Establishment of partnerships with organizations working in the field of carbon trading and sustainable development
- Socialization of developers
- Bring Cardano people / community together - give them an interesting challenge and boundaries - have fun solving
- Bring together a good jury panel
- Build reputation in the ecosystem

### 4. Details of the Proposal
- Hackathon format: Online or offline, depending on feasibility and participant preferences
- Duration: 2-3 days
- Participants: Developers, designers, and experts in the field of carbon trading
- Judging criteria: Innovation, feasibility, impact, and potential for scalability
- Prizes and rewards: To be determined, potentially including funding, mentorship, and support for further development
- Design
  - planning
  - promotion
- The work / proposed solution need not be market ready - the goal is to foster collaboration and generate exposure.

### 5. Impact and Benefits
By hosting this hackathon, Gimbalabs can:
- Provide a platform for developers to learn about and experiment with blockchain-based carbon trading solutions
- Foster innovation and collaboration among participants, leading to the development of open-source projects that promote sustainable development
- Showcase the potential of Cardano as a digital public good, highlighting its capabilities in supporting environmentally-focused use cases
- Contribute to the attainment of the SDGs, particularly Goal 13 (Climate Action), by promoting the development of blockchain-based solutions for carbon trading.

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
- Number of participants and projects developed
- Quality and impact of projects developed
- Feedback from participants and judges
- Establishment of partnerships with organizations working in the field of carbon trading and sustainable development
