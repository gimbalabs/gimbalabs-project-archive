# Fund proposal presentations

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver

**Short Driver:**
In order to bring more proposals and provide an incentive to contribute to Gimbalabs, individuals that prepare and present proposals to the Open Spaces can request and receive ADA from the Gimbalabs Sustain and Maintain Fund12 treasury.

**Long Driver:**
Gimbalabs is committed to empowering individuals to solve meaningful problems by making proposal formation and consent decision-making central to its governance model. This approach emphasizes the importance of sensing tensions and encourages community members to actively participate in shaping the organization's direction. By closely connecting the community to proposals and providing opportunities for volunteers to contribute and receive ADA compensation, Gimbalabs fosters a diverse and engaged community.

## Proposal

### Title
Fund proposal presentations and consent process

### Background or Rationale
Gimbalabs is moving to a new format for the open space on Thursday. Written proposals are encouraged. The group will discuss those written proposals in a structure "proposal formation" process.
- Sustain and Maintain Fund12 proposal has a budgeted treasury of 30K ADA
- A simple way to increase contribution to Gimbalabs is needed
- A simple way to express interest in a topic / proposal and community
- A way to highlight a problem and propose a solution that is related to the Gimbalabs primary driver

### Intended Outcomes
- There is evidence of work and initiatives that documents Gimbalabs activity
- There is acknowledgment and respect of the effort to prepare good proposals
- There is an improvement in the quality and quantity of proposals
- There is a simple and meaningful way to contribute to Gimbalabs community
- There is a robust way to collect ideas from the community
- There is a practical and meaningful space to practice skills of decentralized ecosystems

### Details of the Proposal
If we agree to this proposal, we agree to:
Set aside a certain percentage of the 30k ADA in the treasury to incentivize the creation of proposals. Funds go to the presenter / proposal writer AND to the consent decision making process facilitator.

Proposals are presented at the Open Space and follow a set process. The presenter is compensated a certain amount for drafting the proposal and going thru the process.

**More Details:**
- This would become an incentivized part of the onboarding process
- Would be an excellent use case for the Andamio contributor reward mechanism

**Questions:**
- What is the target for the number of proposals?
- What is the maximum budget for the entire project? (5% - 1500 ADA or 10% 3000)
- What is the amount to compensate each proposal / proposer? (100 ADA for 15-30 proposals, or 200 ADA for 7-15 proposals)
- Should there be a limit on the maximum amount any one individual community member can receive?
- What about an element of randomness or lottery?
- What about poor quality proposals or rewarding quality?

### Impact, Benefits, Outcome
- Improved proposals that make a positive impact in the GL community
- Improved governance and collaboration skills of the community
- Improved way to onboard contributors to the GL community

### Evaluation Criteria
- Did people make proposals?
- Did they use the format?
- Did they present the proposal?
- Did they provide a wallet address?
- Are the funds transferred / received?
- How did the group feel about the proposals?
- Does the reward scheme result in more people bringing proposals forward?

### Timeline
- Ongoing for Thursday Open Spaces
- Payments made in a timely way following the presentations and consent process
- Review project halfway - 2.5 months - Mid November
- Duration - 5 months - Ends in January (inline with the Sustain and Maintain Fund12 proposal)

### Resources Required
- Someone to facilitate the consent process at Open Spaces
- Someone to admin / manage the project
- Someone to review and release the funds
- Someone to research, write and present Proposals
- An amount of ADA / Gimbal Tokens for presenters
- An amount of ADA / Gimbal Tokens for the facilitators
- Using the contribution platform

### Responsibilities
Someone to:
- Explain the process and activity to potential proposers
- Collect the names, payment info, documents and organize them for the community
- Ensure the requirements are met
- Make the payments

Someone to facilitate the consent process at Open Spaces (Open Space facilitator might fill this role, or a person familiar with the proposal)

Someone to admin / manage the project

Someone to review and release the funds

Someone to research, write and present Proposals

### Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies
- "It does start to expand in complexity"
- Quality Over Quantity: This approach may encourage a flood of proposals, diluting the quality of contributions
- Lack of alignment with Gimbalabs Primary Driver
- Not enough proposals
- The admin of the proposals and payments is a chore and there are problems
- People won't come to the Open Spaces
- People who come won't know the process
- If we compensate proposers, should we compensate everyone participating in the proposal process?
- Making it possible for others to participate - Source is a critical role for others to participate
- Do people understand the proposal process (see Sebastian's question about educating proposers)
- Financial Sustainability: Allocating funds from a limited treasury could jeopardize the long-term sustainability
- Potential for Abuse: The system might be exploited by individuals seeking financial gain rather than genuine contributions
