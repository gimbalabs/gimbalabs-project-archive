# Gimbalabs Fall 2025 Activities

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems (with Cardano and Andamio)

## Proposal Driver

**Situation:**
Cardano stakeholders and especially developers are isolated, lack a sense of community, have projects that need support, or need projects they can support.

**Effects:**
As such, junior developers have a hard time finding projects or finding teammates or learning appropriate skills - or when they do, they have to learn in isolation without community support or sense of belonging.

**Relevance:**
Gimbalabs is a place that offers skills training, support and a sense of belonging.
