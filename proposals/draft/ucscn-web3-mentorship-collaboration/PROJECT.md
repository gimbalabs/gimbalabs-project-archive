# Collaboration Proposal for GimbaLabs Education Team to Support UCSCN's Web3 Development Mentorship Program

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver

To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description

Collaboration Proposal for GimbaLabs Education Team to Support UCSCN's Web3 Development Mentorship Program Leading to the June Hackathon

### 2. Background or Rationale

The Unified Cardano Student Club Nigeria (UCSCN) has received funding from Catalyst Fund 13 to facilitate a large-scale hackathon event aimed at onboarding over 1,000 university students across Nigeria into the Cardano ecosystem. The goal is to provide students with essential knowledge and mentorship in Web3 development on Cardano, ensuring they are adequately prepared for the hackathon.

To achieve this, we seek collaboration with the GimbaLabs education team, which is well-regarded for its expertise in Cardano development education. By leveraging the knowledge, mentorship, and community support of GimbaLabs, we can ensure the students receive structured learning, guidance, and the necessary technical foundation to succeed in the hackathon.

### 3. Intended Outcome

- Equip students with practical Web3 development skills on the Cardano blockchain
- Provide structured mentorship and hands-on learning for one month leading up to the hackathon
- Establish a progress-tracking mechanism to assess students' readiness for the hackathon
- Strengthen the relationship between UCSCN and GimbaLabs in fostering education within the Cardano ecosystem
- Ensure a well-prepared and knowledgeable student cohort ready to participate in the hackathon and contribute scalable solutions to Cardano

### 4. Details of the Proposal

We request the GimbaLabs education team to collaborate with UCSCN by:

1. **Providing Technical Mentorship:** Hosting and facilitating weekly mentorship sessions, live coding demonstrations, and hands-on development guidance for students over a one-month period

2. **Developing an Educational Curriculum:** Structuring a 1-month educational roadmap, dividing key topics into weekly modules covering DApp and smart contract development on Cardano

3. **Implementing Selection and Progress Criteria:** Develop a selection process to assess students' progress and identify those who qualify to participate in the hackathon

4. **Scaling the Initiative:** Assisting in accommodating over 1,000 students effectively by leveraging existing GimbaLabs education frameworks and support structures

### 5. Impact and Benefits

- **For Students:** Gain hands-on experience with Cardano development, making them valuable contributors to the blockchain ecosystem
- **For UCSCN:** Strengthens its capacity to mentor students at scale and ensures effective knowledge transfer
- **For GimbaLabs:** Expands its education reach, experience in facilitating conference-level settings for education, attracting more learners to its ecosystem and fostering the development of real-world solutions on Cardano
- **For the Cardano Community:** Encourages adoption and the creation of innovative blockchain solutions, ultimately contributing to the growth of the ecosystem

### 6. Evaluation Criteria

- Number of students successfully completing the mentorship program
- Engagement levels in weekly sessions (attendance, assignments, participation)
- Number of students qualifying for the hackathon
- Quality of projects developed during the hackathon
- Feedback from students, mentors, and stakeholders

### 7. Timeline

- **Week 1:** Student onboarding and foundational lessons on Cardano blockchain
- **Week 2, 3 & 4:** Smart contract development fundamentals and hands-on coding and DApp development tailored to specific project ideas
- **Preparation Week:** Final mentorship, progress tracking, and hackathon preparation
- **Hackathon (June):** Execution of the 4-day hackathon event

### 8. Resources Required

- **Mentors and Facilitators:** Weekly commitment from GimbaLabs educators
- **Educational Materials:** Structured learning modules and documentation
- **Technical Infrastructure:** Access to tools, platforms, and virtual learning environments
- **Student Support:** Assistance in organizing sessions and tracking progress

### 9. Responsibilities

- **UCSCN:** Student recruitment, administrative support, and coordination
- **GimbaLabs:** Mentorship, curriculum development, session facilitation, and technical guidance
- **Students:** Active participation, engagement in learning, and project development

### 10. Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies

- **Risk:** Difficulty in managing and tracking 1,000+ students
  - **Mitigation:** Utilizing conference settings on Zoom or Google Meet and automated tracking tools

- **Risk:** Low student engagement
  - **Mitigation:** Implement incentives such as certifications and prizes for top-performing students

### 11. Review and Adaptation Plan

- Regular feedback collection from students and mentors
- Continuous iteration of mentorship structure based on engagement metrics
- Post-event evaluation to assess effectiveness and areas for future improvement

We are excited about the potential of this collaboration and look forward to working with GimbaLabs to provide students with a transformative learning experience leading up to the hackathon.
