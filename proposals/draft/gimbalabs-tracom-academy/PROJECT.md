## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
**Tracom-Gimbalabs Blockchain Developer Program (Pilot 2026)**

A collaborative pilot to build Kenya's first blockchain development track within a payments academy, combining Gimbalabs' open learning frameworks, Tracom's industry expertise, and a hybrid team of contributors.

### 2. Background or Rationale
Kenya leads in digital finance innovation, yet blockchain development skills remain scarce.

Tracom Academy trains top software talent for payments, banking, and financial services. However, the next generation of secure, interoperable payment systems will require blockchain and smart contract expertise.

**Current gap:**
- Skilled Kenyan blockchain developers are limited
- Companies rely heavily on outsourcing
- No institution integrates blockchain into payments-focused education

Gimbalabs, a global open-source learning lab for Cardano ecosystem provides battle-tested curricula, learning frameworks, and contributor networks. They're uniquely positioned to help build a **locally-owned blockchain education program** that is practical, hands-on, and production-focused.

The Tracom x Gimbalabs pilot bridges this skills gap by combining:
- Tracom's real-world payments expertise
- Gimbalabs' education frameworks and contributor network
- Lewis to act as the project bridge

### 3. Intended Outcome
**Establish Kenya's first blockchain development track within a payment academy.** Deliver it under an exclusive x-months pilot license for Tracom using a scalable curriculum framework that Gimbalabs can replicate globally.

**Program outcomes:**
- Equip Kenyan developers with practical blockchain and smart contract skills
- Strengthen Tracom's talent pipeline and reduce outsourcing costs
- Create a repeatable education model using Andamio for collaboration and delivery
- Generate paid contributor work for the Gimbalabs ecosystem
- Position both organizations as leaders in blockchain-enabled payment innovation

### 4. Details of the Proposal

**Structure:**
- Pilot deployment at Tracom Academy (September 2026 intake)
- 6 month planning and curriculum build phase (Q1-Q2 2026)
- Led by Lewis, who will serve as the bridge between Tracom and Gimbalabs, with support from the team
- Multi-organization curriculum team (Tracom subject matter experts and Gimbalabs contributors)

**Ownership model:**
- Tracom receives exclusive use of the curriculum for x pilot months
- Gimbalabs retains ownership of the underlying framework
- Optional revenue share model for long-term sustainability

**Platform:**
- All collaboration and learning modules built using Andamio (wallet login, tasks, modules, assessments)

### 5. Impact and Benefits

**For Tracom:**
- Kenya's first blockchain payments curriculum
- Premium paid program for students
- Strong internal talent pipeline
- Reduced dependency on outsourcing
- First-mover advantage in blockchain payments training

**For Gimbalabs:**
- Funded for-profit pilot aligned with new policy
- Ownership of a deployable blockchain education framework
- Contributor roles and paid work
- High-value case study and proof-of-concept
- Global replication rights after pilot exclusivity
- Strengthened ecosystem visibility
