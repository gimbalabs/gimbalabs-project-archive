# Gimbalabs African Developers Education Initiative - 2025

**Summary:** Initiative to educate University of Lagos students on Cardano development and smart contracts, featuring hands-on projects, hackathons, and a focus on building a sustainable developer community in Nigeria, with a total budget of 13,000 ADA for various phases of the program.

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
To Consent a Foundational Cardano development and smart contract Education to Gimbalabs.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
A Cardano development and smart contract education to University Students, University of Lagos Nigeria.

### 2. Background or Rationale
The background of this project came about the intention and thoughts on the existence of a Purpose-driven Cardano developer Students community in Nigeria, with Education as the starting point from Gimbalabs leading to a sustainable and valuable developers community.

### 3. Intended Outcome
- Onboard and teach students specifically on Aiken using Andamio platform
- Introduction and uses of various Cardano developer tools and SDKs e.g Mesh
- Students are expected to deploy and build Cardano Smart contract from scratch
- Building real world project - Hackathon

### 4. Details of the Proposal
**Update 2024-10-24:** Students are currently joining this Github repo to express interest: https://github.com/orgs/CardanoDevNG/people

- Developer and coordinator is needed - Emmanuel and Jethro Students of University of Lagos
- Emmanuel Andamio aiken pbl deep dive during 2-weeks onboarding phase
- Targeted number of sixty to seventy students (50-70 Max.)
- Considerably, at the end a **certificate of participation** should be presented to students.
- Hackathon at the end of education session (group of two or three)
- Top three Hackathon projects are gifted with token prizes in ADA
  - Student performance
  - Hackathon

### 5. Impact and Benefits
- **Scalability of Cardano developers:** Increasing the number of Cardano developers globally and promoting the Cardano developing role in Africa especially Nigeria
- **Educational benefits** - Students education in Gimbalabs using andamio sets a solid foundation for a sustainable Developers students community
- **Opportunity to initiate Blockprint** - A Gimbalabs community extension to the African community focused on cardano core development
- **Cardano acceptability in University Students** - Increasing Cardano general Marketing to University Students and subsequently Nigeria as a whole which is a market driven country in its top adoption of cryptocurrency in 2024 after India
- **Gimbalabs promotion to University Students** - Students awareness to Gimbalabs as the a Cardano community impacting Nigeria students in Cardano blockchain development and education.
- Further collaboration and partnership with Gimbalabs and Students developers community in education to new students also collaborative projects workflow.

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
- Dedicated Students participation criteria
- Sustainable education sessions among Students and Educators.
- Outreach to potential audience (Students).
- Continuity in learning and education among students.
- Further evaluation Criteria conducted by participating Educators.

### 7. Timeline
- 2-3 months of teaching time once the project starts
- Launch Date - June 1, 2025

### 8. Resources Required
- Github repo
- Google meet/zoom for live sessions
- Notion for class and assignment management

- If possible a gimbalabs contributor willing to introduce themselves to the students for 5 Mins. online
- High chance of Students playing Asteria, if possible Hydra-Asteria, one of the best ways to introduce blockchain development to developers.
