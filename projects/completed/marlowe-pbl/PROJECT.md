# Marlowe PBL

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
As Marlowe transitions to a community project (its future is 100% in the community's hands), it's critical for the project to facilitate from day zero the growth of a community of DApp builders who adopt Marlowe.

Gimbalabs will collaborate with the Marlowe core team of maintainers to create a PBL learning experience that prioritizes skills-building and project contributions.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
**Marlowe PBL 2025**

### 2. Background or Rationale

- Catalyst F12 has funded Gimbalabs to build a PBL based on the Marlowe language.
- The initial plan, prior to receiving funding, was to build the Marlowe PBL following the Roadmap set out by the Marlowe core maintainer team (led by Professor Simon Thompson) in its Catalyst F12 proposal "Marlowe 2025". However, the proposal was not funded.
- In Catalyst F13, the proposal "Marlowe 2025: Oracle Protocol, Design and Implementation" was funded. That represents a potentially partial implementation of the Marlowe 2025 roadmap, focused specifically on the Oracle milestone. However, the Marlowe team has clearly expressed its intention to continue the revamp and enhancement of Marlowe to its conclusion.

**Key External Dependencies:**

We need to know the status of Marlowe TS SDK + Runtime. In recent conversation with one of our team members in the Marlowe channel of the IOG Discord server, Tomasz, Marlowe core dev, states:
- *"All the work on TS-SDK and the Runtime is not formally covered by the Catalyst but we do because it is critical for the project."*
- *"Please also note that formally the Catalyst work kicks off really now. We just finished onboarding process etc. Additionally our proposal is planned for 12 months and for sure we will try to engage more and more as the project warms up - I'm able to switch to real devel mode on Marlowe from the end of February."*

On the above point Tomasz, Marlowe's core developer, states that Marlowe's proposal is planned for 12 months. Ours is planned for the same time period, allowing us to synchronize our efforts with those of the Marlowe team as needed.

### 3. Intended Outcome
Build a Marlowe PBL that helps onboard devs to the language and create community around it.

### 10. Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies

1. **No progress in the development of the TS-SDK and the Runtime by the Marlowe team.** As highlighted by one of their developers, these are critical components for fulfilling their Catalyst proposal. This situation places the Marlowe team in a position where they must prioritize delivering on these two areas. Notably, their proposal and ours are aligned in terms of timeline - both have a 12-month implementation period and are set to begin around the same time. This synchronization ensures that we can adapt and make necessary adjustments without jeopardizing our own project timeline.

2. **Not having enough contributors to create the lessons of the Outline modules of the course:** One of the advantages for which Andamio was chosen as the platform to host our course is the ability of its Contribution platform to distribute tasks, evaluate contributions, and assign compensation. Such a mechanism will allow us to open the project to new contributors, both in the Gimbalabs Community and in the Cardano Community in general.
