# Which Spaces - January thru June 2025

"The learning experience as the pathway to contribution"

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
Gimbalabs creates spaces where people and learn, explore, build relationships and practice facilitation. We aim to provide consistency and meetings require facilitation. This is a proposal to make a commitment to consistent dates and to pay facilitators for fulfilling their role.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Which Spaces - January thru June 2025

### 2. Background or Rationale
1. Consistent facilitation of weekly spaces allows us to practice and build for emergence.
2. Keep running Open Spaces same time weekly
3. Getting current work done...with an open door
4. Making spaces for other developers in Cardano ecosystem

### 3. Intended Outcome
- Weekly spaces continue to run through May 2025
- Facilitators are compensated
- New best practices continue to emerge
- Public calendar with links to all spaces
  - Internal: like Open Spaces + PBL Live Coding
  - External: e.g. GameChanger

### 4. Details of the Proposal
Pay facilitators 200 ADA per 90 minute live session
Agree upon regular meeting cadence thru May 2025

### 5. Impact and Benefits

**Educational Benefits:**
- **Starting Point in Cardano Technology**: Gave a foundation to understand and engage with the development ecosystem.
- **Teaching Opportunities**: Can be used to teach Cardano development in schools (PPBL and Mesh PBL).
- **Programming Skills Development**: Gained confidence in learning programming, with opportunities to learn and build.

**Community and Networking:**
- **Meeting New People**: Helped meet new people on similar journeys and make friends.
- **Collaborative Environment**: Provides a virtual office-like space and a truly open collaborative area where big conversations can happen and ideas take root.
- **Belonging and Empowerment**: Offers a feeling of belonging to a larger group, empowering members within the community.

**Governance and Management Practices:**
- **Governance Experimentation**: Explored non-hierarchical governance and is the beginning of Gimbalabs' Governance experiment, aligning with Cardano's implementation.
- **S3 Practices**: Opportunities to practice and learn S3 management and governance practices, enhancing understanding and application.

**Problem Solving and Support:**
- **Support When Stuck**: Available help when encountering difficulties with modules.
- **Q&A and Collaboration**: Benefited from live Q&A sessions that facilitate collaboration and problem-solving.

**Ideation and Project Development:**
- **Idea Presentation and Leadership**: Offered chances to present ideas, lead conversations, and develop new projects.
- **Source and Topics Exploration**: Introduced to various topics and themes, encouraging thinking about the source of information and ideas.

**Technological Collaboration:**
- **Mesh Features and Collaboration**: Learned about Mesh features, fostering building collaborations, and creating useful apps.
- **Partnership with Blink Labs**: Engaged in collaborative efforts with external entities like Blink Labs.
