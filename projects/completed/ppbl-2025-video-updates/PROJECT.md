# PPBL 2025 Video-Updates

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
Videos have provided a valuable learning resource for the students of the PPBL 2024. The current video library for the course is incomplete and out of date.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Community governed update of the PPBL-2024 videos

### 2. Background or Rationale
The Community Governed Update of the Gimbalabs PPBL-2024 course on Andamio has been successfully completed. The aim of this project was to update the lesson content, code and examples in order to align with the most recent updates to the Cardano developer experience. Currently, these lessons have either outdated videos or no video at all. This proposal seeks to outline a plan to provide each lesson (where applicable) with a new video relevant to the lesson content.

### 3. Intended Outcome
Of the 40 lessons included in the PPBL-2024 course, 32 have either no video at all, or outdated content. Create a new video for each of these 32 lessons (amended to 16 as per Gimbalabs open spaces sign off).

### 4. Details of the Proposal
This proposal seeks to leverage the Andamio platform to open this task to the wider Gimbalabs community.

**Process:**
- A new project created on Andamio
- Split into 32 different tasks to record a video for each lesson
- Each task tied to an ADA reward
- Prerequisites: Complete the 'get started with gimbalabs' course on Andamio (module 101)
- Each video: brief intro, complete lesson walkthrough, successful task completion

**Roles:**
- **Video Creator** - Records videos for lessons
- **Video Editor** - Adds Gimbalabs branding, posts to YT channel
- **Social Media Coordinator** - Creates posts for every new video
- **Coordinator/Project Lead** - Creates Andamio project, handles treasury, QA

### 5. Impact and Benefits
This would aid Gimbalabs users/students in their learning journey. Plus help to enhance the Gimbalabs brand further, in terms of fresh user facing content, but also in displaying the strength of community led problem solving which is central to the Gimbalabs driver.

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
32 new videos are recorded and placed on the Gimbalabs YouTube channel. A YouTube link can be supplied as task evidence.

### 7. Timeline
2 months

### 8. Resources Required
- Recording software (Awesome screenshot has good free service)
- Notion access for each approved video contributor

**Budget:**
- 16 x 150 ADA for video creators = 2,400 ADA (Community Tasks - Andamio)
- 16 x 100 ADA for video editor = 1,600 ADA (Community Tasks - Andamio)
- Social Media Coordinator = 800 ADA
- Coordinator / Video QA = 1,600 ADA
- ADA returned from PPBL Maintainers Project + 2,000 ADA
- **Total: 6,400 ADA** (12,800 total with extension)

### 9. Responsibilities
- **Video Creator** - Complete 'getting started at gimbalabs' 101 on Andamio, create videos, submit for QA
- **Video Editor** - Edit videos, add branding, post to Gimbalabs YT channel
- **Social Media Coordinator** - Create posts for new videos on Twitter, LinkedIn
- **Coordinator/Project Lead** - Create Andamio project & tasks, handle treasury, manage task Kanban, provide assistance

### 10. Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies
- Content doesn't reach a large enough audience
- Videos not all created inside a 2 month timeframe

## Open Questions and Decisions

Questions addressed during proposal review:

1. **Are there firm commitments for the roles?**
   - Not yet. Jethro as a video editor.

2. **The quality of the videos from general community?**
   - The QA role and the 'evidence' in the Andamio task can address quality - have to meet a standard.

3. **Logistics of transfer of the videos and length of the videos?**
   - Some are short, some longer. LiveCoding sessions up to 90 min. Some are only 10.

4. **For Andamio, is multisig for the review?**
   - Catalyst Milestone Moderation style to review the quality - signoff, not a bottleneck at a single coordinator. Andamio can have multiple project managers to sign off on videos.

5. **Are we doing live coding session for these? Is the workload now only 16 videos?**
   - Yes to live coding for some. 16 to fit into the time frame.

6. **Which 16 videos?**
   - For each lesson. Maintainers (Andrew, Cesar, and Mo) can decide.

7. **Value of using Andamio?**
   - The mission critical 'ness of the course videos.
