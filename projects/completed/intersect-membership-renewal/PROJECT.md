# Intersect membership renewal

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
Intersect membership renewal is coming up. Price is US$ 1,000 and needs to be paid by the 6th of September.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Intersect membership renewal

### 2. Background or Rationale
In the short term, Intersect is emerging as a dominant player in the Cardano ecosystem, with aspects as important as Cardano's budget falling into its domain. Therefore, while over the course of the next year it will become clearer what the true scope of this MBO will be, for the moment the most strategic thing for Gimbalabs seems to be to remain a member with all the rights that membership confers.

Benefits: https://docs.intersectmbo.org/intersect-overview/intersect-membership/membership-tiers-and-benefits

### 3. Intended Outcome
As Cardano's prestigious organization in the Education area (with several of its members participating in various Intersect committees), Gimbalabs will not be left out of the action and will be able to participate extensively in what is happening at Intersect (including the drama, but also including developments in the areas of onboarding and advocacy).

### 4. Details of the Proposal
- A payment of US$1000 must be consented by the Gimbalabs Community.
- If consented, the money should come out of the Gimbalabs treasury and paid directly at the Intersect's members area: https://www.intersectmbo.org/
- Check if the email jdunseith@gimbalabs.io has the login credentials to the original founding member account.

### 5. Impact and Benefits
- Paying for such memberships should be seen both as an operating expense (part of the utilities to be paid) and as an investment (access to markets, customers, partners, grants, etc).
- As an Enterprise Member, Gimbalabs will be able to pursue commercial opportunities and partnerships with Intersect and its community.
- You will not leave your market share to players in the Onboarding and Advocacy area without the reputation and recognition that Gimbalabs has.
- It will buy time for other MBOs (such as PRAGMA) to emerge, once they have a clearer plan for how they will navigate the non-technical reality of community and Governance in which we inevitably live today.

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
Payment to Intersect = US$1000

### 7. Timeline
Payment date: Friday, September 06, 2024.

### 8. Resources Required
- Consent by the Community
- Gimbalabs Treasury funds

### 9. Responsibilities
- Annual Membership Payment
- Start exploring and looking for opportunities on Intersect to get the most out of that investment.

### 10. Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies
- After a year, membership in Intersect does not generate significant business volume: membership is annual, therefore, renewal will be suspended the following year.

### 11. Review and Adaptation Plan
Plan revision: September 2025.
