# Gimbalabs 2025 - Sustaining and/or Maintaining

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
*Current situation, effects of that, and the relevance to the driver above*

Gimbalabs has a new set of funded projects, a backlog of work, and opportunities to build toward sustainability for a group of committed contributors in 2025. How can we balance the risk we're taking on with the potential benefits?

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Gimbalabs 2025 - Sustaining and/or Maintaining

### 2. Background or Rationale
Gimbalabs is creating exciting opportunities for new and experienced contributors to Gimbalabs. We have the opportunity to create sustainable opportunities for [a number] of people. In order to embrace this opportunity, we must also share a reasonable amount of risk where this is still uncertainty.

### 3. Intended Outcome
Gimbalabs has a group of contributors who are building toward sustainability while sharing the risks and liabilities associated with the honest pursuit of our opportunities.

### 4. Details of the Proposal
1. Settling Gimbalabs debt + reserving for liabilities
2. Starting 2025 by using Open Spaces for Driver Mapping workshops
3. Future proposal: sharing pledge and rewards for Gimbal Pool

### 5. Impact and Benefits
- Gimbalabs has a chance to pursue not only "maintenance" but also "sustainability"
- Moving forward with a shared commitment to shared goals

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
- Does maintenance of Gimbalabs feel sustainable for people contributing to its success?
- Do contributors trust each other and trust that any risk they're taking on is worthwhile?
