# Send Steve to DREP DBATE

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Send Steve to DREP DBATE

### 2. Background or Rationale
Gimbalabs needs representation at the dRep debate in Fort Myers to enhance visibility, strengthen connections with other dReps, and deepen the understanding of the dRep role. This event also provides a valuable opportunity for Steve to engage directly with the community.

From Steve:
*I feel it is a nice way for Gimbalabs to begin to engage in the ongoing discussion in the Cardano community about DReps and how they self-represent as DReps, as well to test out this initial attempt Intersect is making to facilitate dialogue/debate between DReps.*

*All the relevant information for planning is on the Luma link [below]. If anyone in the Gimbalabs community would like to go I would support the expense. If no one else is able to go but supports the idea, I have the time to go. For me, travel, hotel etc would entail close to $1000, but should not exceed that amount.*

### 3. Intended Outcome
- Gimbalabs is effectively represented at the event.
- Networking opportunities with other dReps are leveraged.
- A better understanding of the dRep role and its nuances is achieved.
- ***note from Steve***: There are some very nuanced discussions going on regarding the ratification of the Constitution signed in Argentina. My main reason for going is to get some more of that in-person understanding of the differing viewpoints. With this experience, I hope that the GL community will feel more informed from one of their own having the opportunity to engage in discussions with others in the community more directly involved with the process than any of us have.
- Steve benefits from professional and personal growth, including the opportunity to travel and represent Gimbalabs.
