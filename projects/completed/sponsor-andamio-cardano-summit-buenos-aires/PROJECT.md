# Sponsor Andamio's Attendance at Cardano Summit Buenos Aires (Argentina)

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
After developing a Cardano consumer-driven project, the natural next step is to scale them by pushing them to the market. However, within the largely virtual environment where the Cardano developer community typically operates, opportunities for real-time interaction and meaningful user feedback are limited.

This creates a gap between product development and market validation, slowing down the learning cycles required to build commercially viable solutions.

For low-budget, community-driven, Cardano native projects like Andamio, born out of the Gimbalabs ecosystem, participation in renowned live events—such as the annual Cardano Summit—provides an invaluable opportunity to engage directly with users, partners, and stakeholders.

Documenting and sharing the lessons learned from these real-world interactions strengthens the collective knowledge base of the Gimbalabs community, equipping current and future Gimbalabs cohorts of developer projects with practical insights into how to launch, promote, and commercialize consumer-driven solutions more effectively, and incentivize the passion for sharing the own work with the world.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Sponsor Andamio's Attendance at Cardano Summit Buenos Aires (Argentina)

### 2. Background or Rationale
As a community-driven organization focused on strengthening the environment in which Cardano ecosystem developers build projects that can generate both public benefit and advance the broader mission of enabling others to build and launch their own initiatives, this proposal falls squarely within the scope of Gimbalabs.

The Cardano ecosystem is currently at a pivotal stage: exploring the most effective pathways for projects developed within the ecosystem to become viable beyond it, with the corresponding potential to onboard new users, builders, Web2 organizations, and businesses.

Achieving this objective requires independent projects to experiment, engage externally, and develop a shared knowledge base on how to bring Cardano-native solutions to the world in the form of tangible products.

Contributing back to that knowledge base is not only a responsibility but also the most valuable contribution these projects can make to the ecosystem.

The launch and promotion stage has long been a structural gap within the Cardano ecosystem. Leveraging its experience as an incubator for a substantial number of developer projects, Gimbalabs can play a key role in closing this gap and strengthening the ecosystem's capacity for real-world adoption.

**Why Andamio is suitable:**
- Gimbalabs-incubated project: Its co-founders have been actively involved in various Gimbalabs building spaces for years. Several of them graduated from the first cohorts of the Plutus PBL program and later assumed key community roles — including instructors, builders, facilitators, and onboarders.
- While the initial codebase was developed within the Gimbalabs ecosystem, over time the project gradually charted its own independent path in pursuit of sustainability.
- Over the past two and a half years, since its founding, the project has shown publicly a team behind, consistently maintaining and innovating the codebase, and fostering new collaborations, partnerships and sinergies with other players (devs, teams, founding entities) in the Cardano ecosystem and beyond.
- The project has shown openness and readiness to welcome Gimbalabs community talent to contribute and get involved in the core of the stack. As the project moves toward becoming a fully open-source resource, this collaborative dynamic is expected to grow even stronger.
- The project is looking for ways of growing and creating impact for the benefit of the Cardano ecosystem.

### 3. Intended Outcome
- Showcase Andamio in the Cardano Summit Buenos Aires in the following scenarios: public speakings, interviews, roundtables, commercial showcases.
- Bringing on Andamio the attention of key stakeholders in the Cardano ecosystem, and new public in general.
- Create opportunities for partnerships and collaborations with the attendees to the event.
- **Documenting in a public Gitbook or similar tooling, the learnings, methodologies, approaches developed.**
- Andamio beneficiaries of this proposal will become mentors in the area of market promotion for current and future projects with market awareness and aspirations.
