## Original Proposal

### Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

### Proposal Driver

**Content creation always seems to be a bottleneck.** Cardano development content is scarce, always out of date, and expensive - and yet at Gimbalabs we have a history of building content teams. Now, we have an opportunity to upgrade our systems of delivering great dev-facing content while building for a high-impact, funded project.

The Fund 13 Proposal [Decentralized Domain Name System (DNS) Marketplace powered by Cardano and Handshake blockchains](https://projectcatalyst.io/funds/13/cardano-open-developers/decentralized-domain-name-system-dns-marketplace-powered-by-cardano-and-handshake-blockchains) needs CLI developers. Mix and Adrian are writing the on-chain code. At least 20k ADA in rewards are available for the project. This is a great opportunity to update a PBL course with the goal of onboarding devs to a specific project.

Gimbalabs has TypeScript skills but limited Go experience. A draft Cardano Go PBL course exists but needs updates. This proposal addresses both bottlenecks: update the course so that it's ready to onboard DNS CLI developers **and** build systems for getting agentic help with writing great content.

We'll partner with Blink Labs, Mix, and Adrian to create a replicable methodology for contributor onboarding content. Not just writing—working with teams to build content that actually onboards.

**Strategic Context**:
- CF securing .ada/.cardano domains; Gimbalabs owns .cardano on Handshake
- Course update = testing ground for agentic content methodology
- Real market need: systematic solutions for contributor onboarding

### Proposal

**1. Title**
Update Cardano Go PBL course to onboard developers for DNS CLI development (Catalyst Milestone 4)

**2. Background**
**Why Go Matters**: Go is everywhere in Web2 infrastructure. Blink Labs is leading the way in bringing it to Cardano, and they understand the implications. Cardano Go integrations are needed in enterprise projects. Gimbalabs needs Go literacy.

**DNS Marketplace**: Cross-chain infrastructure connecting Handshake domains to Cardano smart contracts. Mix and Adrian are writing the on-chain code through Catalyst. Milestone 1 is complete and M2 will wrap up soon. [Milestone 4 (CLI)](https://milestones.projectcatalyst.io/projects/1300082/milestones/4) needs developers. Let's train and onboard those developers.

**The Gap**: Existing Go course needs streamlining and DNS CLI examples.

**Course**: https://app.andamio.io/course/fd28cf17d1869bcb1f1f3ceaa7daf02d14358ba74691fd679ba3b633
