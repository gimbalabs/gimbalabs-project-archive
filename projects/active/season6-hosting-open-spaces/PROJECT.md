## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver

### Driver: Gimbalabs Open Spaces (Thursday Governance Sessions)

**Situation:**
As the number of Gimbalabs projects and contributors grows, so does the need for a consistent space where coordination, alignment, and decision-making can occur transparently and inclusively. Without a regular rhythm for these conversations, communication fragments, decisions slow down, and the sense of shared purpose can fade.

**Effect:**
When community members lack a shared forum to stay connected, opportunities for collaboration and mutual support are missed. Projects risk becoming siloed, contributors may feel disconnected or uninformed, and Gimbalabs' collective intelligence remains underutilized.

**Relevance:**
Regular Open Spaces provide a vital heartbeat for Gimbalabs governance. These sessions offer a transparent and participatory environment where members can share updates, discuss emerging needs, co-create proposals, and practice consent-based decision-making. By maintaining this rhythm, Gimbalabs strengthens alignment with its **primary driver** — to provide open, replicable, and safe spaces to learn, explore, and empower individuals and organizations anywhere to solve meaningful problems.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Conduct Open Spaces / Gimbalabs governance activities

### 2. Background or Rationale
These Open Spaces sessions - typically conducted on Thursday at 14:30 UTC - are fundamental to the Gimbal Labs operation. They are the primary way that we decide what to support and to fund - they answer the question "How do we want to spend our time".

They are an example of the Gimbal lab style of Sociocracy 3 governance - which includes check in and check out rounds, proposal formation, and consent based decision making.

### 3. Intended Outcome
Keep the Open Spaces governance sessions going

### 4. Details of the Proposal
Each session includes:
- Host and facilitate zoom call in the Gimbalabs / S3 style
- Maintain the Notion Project board
- Maintain the Notion Agenda board
- Youtube posts with descriptions
- Social Media posts with images
- Discord Announcements

Conduct 13 sessions between Sept and Dec 2025
