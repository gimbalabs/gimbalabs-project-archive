# Notion Plus Accounts for Gimbalabs Contributors

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
In 2024, Gimbalabs has written a organizational driver, defined five Circles, and is starting to look at specific projects. Along the way, we organized the Gimbalabs Discord server to align loosely with the PARA method. Now, we have some funds that can be used for projects, and independent activity is starting to take root. **We need a way to organize our work, track tasks, and facilitate collaboration.** It's time to implement a project management system that works for Gimbalabs Circles. When we do, contributors will be able to track projects, prioritize work, and complete impactful projects.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Notion Plus Accounts for Gimbalabs Contributors

### 2. Background or Rationale
Notion (https://www.notion.so/pricing) is a flexible platform with robust project-management features that is well suited to [Sociocracy 3.0](https://sociocracy30.org/) (S3) patterns and the [PARA method](https://www.buildingasecondbrain.com/para). The Andamio team is using it, and can bring the structures we've refined to Gimbalabs.

### 3. Intended Outcome
Create paid Notion Plus accounts for Gimbalabs contributors. Create a Gimbalabs Teamspace that is organized with Project Tracking, Circles Pages, and internal documentation.

### 4. Details of the Proposal
- $96 per user/year - this proposal is to make a commitment to using Notion for a year.
- This proposal is to use Gimbal Pool rewards to pay for Notion accounts for Gimbalabs Contributors
- The Education Circle can take the lead on setting up the Teamspace
- Together, we can define what qualifies a "Contributor" for a Notion account

### 5. Impact and Benefits
- Build alignment for Gimbalabs Circles
- Apply S3 Project Proposal patterns
- We'll have a clear Project tracking board that everyone can use to set priorities
- Notion Project and Task links can be added to Andamio Project listings on-chain
- Any Notion documents can be published for public sharing. This makes it easy to move between private and public work.

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
- After 6 months, are Gimbalabs Contributors using this Notion Teamspace?
- In September, 2024: Ask everyone who has used Notion at Gimbalabs whether it worth continuing to use it.
- In March, 2025: Choose to renew or cancel subscriptions

### 7. Timeline
- March 2024: Set up Notion accounts + Teamspace. Onboard team members with Notion Plus accounts
- Q2 2024: Practice writing proposals and organizing work

### 8. Resources Required
- $96 USD per year per account
  - From GimbalPool rewards
- Education Circle time
  - Escrow Validator

### 9. Responsibilities
- James will collect email addresses and make accounts
- Education Circle will take the lead on setting up the Teamspace, and share with the rest of the Gimbalabs team

### 10. Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies
- Notion might not catch on with the team or add any efficiencies.
- If this happens, we will not renew the subscriptions in the future.
