# Which Spaces: F14

## Gimbalabs Primary Driver
To provide open, replicable safe spaces to learn, to explore, and to empower individuals and organizations anywhere so that we can solve meaningful problems

## Proposal Driver
Gimbalabs creates spaces where people and learn, explore, build relationships and practice facilitation. We aim to provide consistency and meetings require facilitation. This is a proposal to make a commitment to consistent dates and to pay facilitators for fulfilling their role.

## Proposal

### 1. Title or Brief Description
Which Spaces - November thru March 2025 (December is a vacation month for Gimbalabs)

### 2. Background or Rationale
1. Consistent facilitation of weekly spaces:
   - Allows us to practice and build for emergence
   - Gives learners a set schedule
2. Keep running Open Spaces same time weekly
3. Making spaces for other developers in Cardano ecosystem

### 3. Intended Outcome
- Weekly spaces continue to run through March 2026
- Facilitators are compensated
- New best practices continue to emerge
- Public calendar with links to all spaces
  - Internal: like Open Spaces + PBL Live Coding

### 4. Details of the Proposal

**Spaces being proposed and suggested times:**

**Open Spaces**
- Time: Thursdays at 14:30 UTC

**Community Code Review:** A space to audit open source Aiken code line by line.
- Time: Wednesday at 14:30 UTC

**Mesh Live Coding (Call for facilitators):** An SDK that connects web2 apps to Cardano
- Time: Tuesdays at 14:30 UTC

**Gimbalabs x 45B:** 45B's entrepreneurial spaces will be held at Gimbalabs.
- Time: The timing will be decided once the cohort is assembled to cater to their time zones.
- 18 sessions
- 45B is funded by Catalyst for conducting sessions with aspiring entrepreneurs to get them started on their journeys
- Gimbalabs will support the initiative by providing:
  - Social media attention on X: Posting updates about Gimbalabs x 45B sessions, helping find the cohort through X, and updating the Cardano community on cohorts' journey
  - Posting the narrative video on a new playlist on Gimbalabs channel called 'Gimbalabs x 45B'
  - Providing artistic support: Provide a small budget of 150 ADA/week for a team of two directors who will direct the series. The directors' job is to attend all 45B sessions and build narratives on each of the entrepreneurs. They will be working with 45B's editor each week to create a short clip/clips of that week's session.

**Other possible spaces:**
- Tech Standup
- Live Coding in support of PBLs
- Space in support of the Gimbalabs dRep activities
- Playground
- Space in support of a Gimbalabs funded Catalyst Proposal - like GIMBALABS x VESPR

Note: The criteria for a space to be hosted is that a facilitator/s should take responsibility for the space and it is consented by the Gimbalabs community.

### 5. Impact and Benefits

**Educational Benefits:**
- **Starting Point in Cardano Technology**: Gives a foundation to understand and engage with the development ecosystem through Aiken and Mesh.
- **Teaching Opportunities**: Gives the opportunity to earn by teaching/facilitation a technical space
- **Programming Skills Development**: Helps attendees and facilitators confidence in learning programming, with opportunities to learn and build.

**Community and Networking:**
- **Meeting New People**: Meet new people on similar journeys and make friends.
- **Collaborative Environment**: Provides a virtual office-like space and a truly open collaborative area where big conversations can happen and ideas take root.
- **Belonging and Empowerment**: Offers a feeling of belonging to a larger group, empowering members within the community.

**Governance and Management Practices:**
- **Governance Experimentation**: Explore non-hierarchical governance and is the beginning of Gimbalabs' Governance experiment, aligning with Cardano's implementation.
- **S3 Practices**: Opportunities to practice and learn S3 management and governance practices, enhancing understanding and application.

**Problem Solving and Support:**
- **Q&A and Collaboration**: Live Q&A sessions that facilitate collaboration and problem-solving.

**Ideation and Project Development:**
- **Idea Presentation and Leadership**: Offered chances to present ideas, lead conversations, and develop new projects.
- **Source and Topics Exploration**: Introduced to various topics and themes, encouraging thinking about the source of information and ideas.

**Technological Collaboration:**
- **Mesh Features and Collaboration**: Learned about Mesh features, fostering building collaborations, and creating useful apps.
- **Partnership with Blink Labs**: Engaged in collaborative efforts with external entities like Blink Labs.

### 6. Evaluation Criteria
- Attendance + views on YT
- Attendees/facilitators that go on to build solutions
- Number of proposals that pass through consent process
