# Follow Gimbalabs Proposal Process

- Step - Come up with an idea
- Check - Do you have a Discord account and joined GL server?
- Step - Talk to folks in the Discord about the idea
    - bring up the idea at meeting -
- Step - Write the idea down in the proposal format
    - ‣
- Check - Do you have a Notion account?
    - Step - Create a Notion account
- Step - Create the proposal in notion (this may be an issue if you don't have an notion account) you will need a sponsor with a notion account or request a notion account which is a separate process step four is
- Check - Do you know the Consent Decision Making process?
- Step - Present the idea at Open Space. Check for understanding and feedback
- Step - Review and revise the proposal
- Step - Schedule and conduct the Consent Decision making process
